并购案例 ：并购日本本间高尔夫球杆制造公司（2009.08–2011.09）

在2010 年成立规模为5500万美元的专项目基金(科瑞中国基金Ⅰ)、收购由与株式会社本间高尔夫球杆制造(HONMA GOLF CO., LTD.) 两家日本股东。HONMA 公司的控制股权。日本本间高尔夫球杆制造公司始创于1959年，1978年改组设立为株式会社本间。日本本间于2004年12月在株式会社Jasdaq证券交易所上市。公司拥有先进的材料及制造技术及HONMA品牌完善的营销网络和专卖店， 并购后，国内浙江一位股东收购了HONMA公司并布局了全国销售等。

HONMA Golf Co., Ltd. whose headquarter was in Tokyo, Japan is a well-known brand name for 50 years, specializing in manufacturing and retail of golf clubs and other golf-related products worldwide. HONMA was listed on Jasdaq on Dec 2014. The acquisition was made by a special purpose M&A fund domiciled in Cayman Island but managed through Hong Kong. HONMA was sold to a Chinese consortium which later set up the sales management headquartered in Shanghai for China.

并购案例：德国慕尔瀚国际集团（Muehlhan AG）及美国SIPCO表面处理工程公司

1997-1999年，林先生参与了总部位于美国休斯顿的 Surface Protection, Inc. 与美林银行(Merrill Lynch Investment Bank) 并购与整合在德国、迪拜、马来西亚及新加坡收购大型除锈工程服务公司。在该项并购当中，与美林银行组织了3亿美元作为并购基金，在收购时，并购了德国的上市公司Muehlhan AG（慕尔瀚国际集团）及美国SIPCO表面处理公司以及Haraco-Singapore公司，Haraco-Dubai公司等大型船务工程服务公司。慕尔瀚国际集团成立于1881年，至今已有130年的历史，在欧洲共有20多家分支机构，1000多名员工。集团专业从事船舶、海洋工程及其他钢结构工程的防腐涂装，在国际上享有极高的声誉。 2006年10月，慕尔瀚国际集团在Frankfurt Stock Exchange（法兰克福证券交易所）上市，并购了前期被收购的美国SIPCO表面处理公司以及Haraco亚洲船务工程服务公司。

M&A Case Study: Acquisition of Technology Services Companies.

In 1997-1999 years, Mr. Lim was part of the M&A Team of Houston-based, Surface Protection, Inc. which set up a US$300 million to acquire corrosion protection services companies with intention to deploying its latest Newjet technology. Merrill Lynch Bank was the advisory and underwriting bank, that helped the acquisition of the German listed company Muehlhan AG, US-SIPCO and Haraco-Singapore, Haraco-Dubai company and other FSO/FSPO engineering services company.

Muehlhan international group was founded in 1881, has been 130 years of history, specializing in anti-corrosion coatings for ships, ocean engineering and other steel structures, and enjoys a high reputation in the world. In October 2006, Mulhhlhan was listed in Frankfurt Stock Exchange and the entities using the Newjet technology were acquired.

并购案例：上海光桥科技公司（Photonics Bridge，Inc.）

在这项并购当中，林先生系光桥科技公司的董事，代表大华银行-深创投美元基金及所有基金投资者，负责了与西门子谈判，并于2006年处理了光桥科技被西门子并购。光桥科技是一家领先的生产运营级光传输产品的制造商，产品已经行销全世界50多个国家和地区。该公司掌握业界领先并具有完全自主知识产权的光通信核心技术。其MetroWave新一代光网络系列产品在整体设计、系统功能、先进器件的引用和设备稳定性等方面都处于业界领先水平。

Acquisition case: Shanghai Photonics Bridge (Inc.)

In this merger, Mr. Lim represented the investors’ group, comprising of UOB Bank-SZCG and a as board members of Photonic Bridge, Inc. in the acquisition negotiation with SIEMENS, and the deal was consummated in 2006. Photonics Bridge is a leading manufacturer of advanced optical transmission products which was sold in more than 50 countries and regions around the world. The company MetroWave new generation optical network products was the leading product in the overall design, system function, advanced device reference and equipment stability.
