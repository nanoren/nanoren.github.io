创始合伙人:钱银虎

  • 2017/12/16
  • 合伙人介绍

Tiger was a Founding Partner of Shanghai Financial Information Services Co. Ltd. and Yantie （Singapore） Pte. Ltd., and a co-founder of e-commerce platform, Nine Qian Ho Agricultural Products Co., Ltd.

From 2011 to 2014, Tiger was Center Manager for HNA Cloud Commercial Bank Cooperation Center, responsible for 3rd party payments and oversees partnerships with major Chinese financial institutions (achieving 3rd party online payment of 80 billion RMB per annum).

From 2009 to 2011, Tiger was Head of the Market Management Dept. for Shanghai Bulk Agricultural Products Electronic Trading Centre. Responsible for bulk commodity e-trading platform, online supply chain finance business, responsible for the innovative design, variety management and offline channel management of bulk agricultural products in Henan, Gansu, Guangdong and other areas with products transaction size of more than 90 billion RMB per annum.

In the year of 2008, Tiger was Director of a Japanese pharmaceutical chiral-technology company based in PRC, in-charge of research and realized market penetration of 23 key intermediates of chiral medicines.

Tiger graduated from Fudan University (Master of Organic Chemistry); Lanzhou University (Bachelor of chemistry). Tiger’s Scientific Achievements include appointment by the National Natural Science Foundation of China, the Shanghai Science and Technology Special Fund and engaged in the Shanghai City Board of Education Special Fund.

Tiger is a qualified manager for Fund Management （Asset Management Association of China）

YS is a Founding Partner of Shanghai Yantie Financial Services Co., Ltd., Yantie （Singapore）Pte. Ltd., Ever Zen Investment (HK) Ltd

YS had over 30 years of operational management and venture investments experiences. He has over 20 years experience as a venture capital, private equity, fund-of-funds and investment advisory experiences. YS operates his venture businesses in the PRC since the year 2000. Prior to being in the PRC, YS was the General Manager of NSTB Holdings Pte. Ltd., which managed a US$1 billion fund-of-funds, 2 venture capital funds and 3 business angel funds (AUM: approx. US$1.5 billion) under the Singapore government-initiated techno-entrepreneurship (TIF) program. YS was also the founding member and partner of the Venture TDF Group, was General manager for United Overseas Bank (Singapore)’s UOB Shenzhen Venture Fund and a founding partner of Shenzhen Orica Capital Co., Ltd., a managing partner of Creat Capital (HK) Limited and partner of Mercury Capital Investment (Shanghai) and Partner of Yafo Capital (Shanghai).

Some of the recent successful investments include: Sinovel (CH:601558), Invengo (SHE:002161), Techfaith Wireless (NASDAQ:CNTF), Ninetown (NASDAQ:NINE); SMIC (NYSE:SMI & HKEX: 0981); Sina (NASDAQ:SINA); Alibaba (HKEX:1688); Liquidmetal (OTCBB); China Netcom (NYSE:CN & HKEX:0906); New Summit Bio (CRO，OTCBB); Vital Technology Pte Ltd (M&A,HP Inc.),Photonic Bridge, Inc. (M&A, Siemen);HONMA Golf (Japan) Co., Ltd. (M&A) and other corporate financings deals. PE Funds Advisory Services include Tripod Capital, STIC, 3VSourceOne，Pan Pacific Ventures.

Prior to being an investment manager, YS worked with the Singapore Aerospace (AVP, Business Development), Perth Aerospace Pty Ltd (Country Manager) and Surface Protection, Inc. (VP/GM, Asia Operation)， Nestle （Singapore)

YS graduated with a B Eng (Mech Eng, 2nd Class Upper) from Glasgow University, UK and a MBA (Investment & Finance) from Hull University, UK.

大别山九千禾农产品电商平台联合创始人。

曾担任海航云商银行合作中心经理，负责第三方支付金融机构通道合作（其中第三方线上支付规模达800亿元/年）、大宗商品电子交易平台在线供应链金融业务等。

毕业于复旦大学（有机化学硕士）；兰州大学（化学学士）。

2003年至2006年，曾从事的《带鸟嘌呤功能基团的有机电致发光材料的设计、合成与机理研究》研究项目，获得了国家自然科学基金（60325412、90406021和50428303）以及上海市科委专项基金（03DZ11016和04XD14002）以及上海市教委专项基金（2003SG03）等基金项目的资助。

2003年3月，曾参与了应用有机化学国家重点实验室 (兰州大学)组织的《毛叶香茶菜二萜类提取物的分离及结构鉴定》的研究工作。
