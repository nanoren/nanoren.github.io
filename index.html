业务类型

  • 2017/12/16
  • 业务类型

三大业务板块

财务顾问：接受客户的委托和要求，并根据客户的财务状况以及发展目标，提供财务规划、融资结构设计、潜在客户或投资人的尽值调查，并设计专属的融资方案等，我司收取财务顾问费。主要业务包括，境外投资管理 、基金融资管理、股权投资、财务顾问、资本运作等。

Financial Advisory : Provides financial services and solutions, including, financing structuring and plans, to meet with customers’ requirements, overseas investment management, strategic partnership, fund management, equity investment, financial consultant, capital operation and other aspects of the project financing, etc.

股权投资基金管理：通过发起、募集、管理以及投后管理基金等不同的环节，对标的项目进行阶段的股权融资、可转化债券融资等方式，重点支持符合国家战略发展的行业。

Fund Management: Co-partnering with GPs, Sourcing of LPs, serving as venture partners and other private equity and opportunistic investments and management.

资产管理及并购重组：接受客户的委托和要求，并根据客户的资产状况以及发展需求，并以市场为导向，为客户定制资产提升、资产再造解决方案，以及为客户兼并收购境内外上市公司（或非上市公司）物色筛选目标公司；并提供尽职调查、资产评估、财务规划、制订实施并购方案等。

Asset management and mergers and Acquisitions: customized according to the customer’s assets status and development needs, design and execute market-oriented solutions for customer’s asset improvement, as well re-engineering schemes such as mergers and acquisitions of domestic and foreign listed companies (or non-listed companies); provides due diligence, asset evaluation and financial planning and implement the acquisition plan etc..
