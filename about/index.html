盐铁金融

“盐铁”一词源自于中国古代一部重要的政治经济学著作《盐铁论》。该著作是由西汉的桓宽根据著名的“盐铁会议”记录整理而成的重要史书，书中记述了发生在汉昭帝始元六年，政府高层和民间人士代表对汉武帝时期的政治、经济、军事、外交、文化的一场政策大辩论。这场会议所迸发出的智慧火花，对现代社会发展仍然具有重要的借鉴意义。

“Yantie” comes from an important political economics book known as “Salt-Iron Ideology”. This book, complied by Huan Kuan of West Han Dynasty recorded how economic theory and methods influences political ideology in the early days when Han Zhaodi ruled for 6 years. The ideology was propounded at the “Salt-Iron Conference” where political, economic, military, diplomatic and cultural effects on development of a society were debated. It helps us explore the challenges and possibilities in educating ourselves in an increasingly globalized world.

盐铁金融的服务宗旨在于：结合中国古代传统智慧与现代金融理念，以“创始合伙人+项目合伙人”为架构，构建一个开放、公平、高效的创业集合体，为社会创造价值。

Yantie Financial Service’s tenet is: Installing Chinese ancient traditional wisdom and modern financial concept into our partnerships, construct an open, fair and equitable business environment that benefits our business partners and the society as a whole.

为积极响应国家“一带一路”倡议，上海盐铁公司在新加坡设立盐铁关联公司，共同服务于中国企业、项目或资本的“走出去”以及海外公司、优质项目或海外基金的“走进来”。

In line with the Chinese “Belt & Road Initiative” we had established both offices in Shanghai and Singapore to service, both our Chinese and overseas clients, in their endeavor on “Moving Beyond China” and “Moving Into China” for their financing needs for projects and investments.
